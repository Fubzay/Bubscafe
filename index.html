Our Story

A Little Spot With a
Big Heart

Bub's Cafe has been an Elizabethtown institution for over thirty years. Tucked away on W. Poplar Street, we're the kind of place where the waitstaff knows your order before you sit down and the coffee's always hot.

Everything we serve is made fresh — from our famous thick-cut bacon and fluffy omelets to our hand-pressed burgers that keep the lunch crowd coming back. No shortcuts, no freezer bags. Just real, honest food cooked with care.
